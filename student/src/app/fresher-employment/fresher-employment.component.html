Fresher Employment Centre


As a part of the Corporate Social Responsibility, ITTech has established Freshers' Employment Center, a platform that will ensure a win-win situation for freshers and recruiters alike. It will be an organized effort for freshers seeking employment and companies seeking recruits. Freshers can enroll with FEC and would be provided orientation and kept posted about opportunities at no cost. So also, companies can register with FEC to hire fresh talent that has been screened by ITTech based on the projected requirements.


Desired Qualification

BE / BTech / MCA / MCS / MSc / BCA / BSc / BCS / MBA etc.


Highlights

    Free Job Assistance with 3200+ clients
    Free Workshops and Sessions by Experts
    Free Job Alerts
    Free Technology updates & many more...

Fill in the registration form online given in the link below : FresherEmploymentCenter

Fresher Candidates who have filled in the registration form online will be contacted by email by ITTech within 2 weeks. All students those who get the emails will be required to visit ITTech Karve Road Pune office as per the schedule given to them in the email to verify the documents and to appear for Assessment test (Aptitude & Technical test) to qualify for further Free Job Assistance.

NOTE:

    All the services under Fresher Employment Centre are voluntary and free of cost.
    Currently, this service is available for Jobs in IT industry only.
    ITTech reserves all the rights to modify, withdraw, make any changes in the same without any prior intimation.
    ITTech do not guarantee any interview opportunities, jobs under this initiative.
    We do not charge any fees for registrations and neither any fees after getting Job.
    Preference for all job openings will be given to candidates who have undergone training programs from ITTech.
    No Phone calls regarding the same will be entertained.
